On November 19, 2025, a special prayer program was held in Dharamsala, India, where over 240 monks and nuns from three monasteries in Taiwan gathered to recite Je Tsongkhapa's "Essence of True Eloquence" (Lekshe Nyingpo) in Tibetan.
<br><br>

![Monks and nuns watching His Holiness arrive](../../assets/activity-recite/2025-11-19-Dharamsala-G01_SA10524.jpg)
Monks and nuns from three monasteries in Taiwan are watching His Holiness the Dalai Lama arrive to attend a prayer program in the hall of his residence in Dharamsala, HP, India, on November 19, 2025.

![Offering traditional gifts to His Holiness](../../assets/activity-recite/2025-11-19-Dharamsala-G02_SA10568.jpg)
