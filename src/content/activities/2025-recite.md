<p style="font-size: 1.5rem; color: #666; text-align: left;">On November 19, 2025, a special prayer program was held in Dharamsala, India, where over 240 monks and nuns from GEBIS and GWBI gathered to recite Je Tsongkhapa's "Essence of True Eloquence" (Lekshe Nyingpo) in Tibetan.</p>
<br><br>

![Monks and nuns watching His Holiness arrive](../../assets/activity-recite/2025-11-19-Dharamsala-G01_SA10524.jpg)
<p style="font-size: 1.5rem; color: #666; text-align: left;">Monks and nuns are watching His Holiness the Dalai Lama arrive to attend a prayer program in the hall of his residence in Dharamsala.</p>

<br><br>

![Offering traditional gifts to His Holiness](../../assets/activity-recite/2025-11-19-Dharamsala-G02_SA10568.jpg)
<p style="font-size: 1.5rem; color: #666; text-align: left;">At the commencement of the prayer ceremony, Venerable Qing Cheng, representing GEBIS, presented traditional gifts to His Holiness.</p>

<br><br>

![His Holiness participating in recitation](../../assets/activity-recite/2025-11-19-Dharamsala-G03_SA10600.jpg)
<p style="font-size: 1.5rem; color: #666; text-align: left;">The Dalai Lama participated in the recitation of Tsongkhapa's "Essence of Truth" in Tibetan at the hall in his residence in Dharamsala.</p>

<br><br>

![Chanting the Essence of Truth](../../assets/activity-recite/2025-11-19-Dharamsala-G04FUJI9904.jpg)
<p style="font-size: 1.5rem; color: #666; text-align: left;">His Holiness the Dalai Lama and over 240 monks and nuns chanted Tsongkhapa's "The Essence of Truth" in the Tibetan language.</p>
