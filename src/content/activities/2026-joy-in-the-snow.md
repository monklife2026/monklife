<p>After the successful conclusion of the Five Major Treatises exam on January 30th, with two weeks remaining until Chinese New Year's Eve (February 16th),
the monks had a sudden inspiration: Let's create some joy in the snow, adding festive cheer and laughter to PEI.</p>
<p>Thus, over 100 monks braved sub-zero temperatures for three consecutive days,
shoveling and sculpting snow by hand to create a snowman world that invites people to pause, smile, and wander.</p>
